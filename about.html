Our Mission

Good sound isn't
a luxury.

"18 years behind the desk taught me that good sound isn't just technical — it's about understanding the room, the congregation, and the purpose."

Bolux Audio Systems was founded by Kingsley Boluwaji Akintewe, a professional live sound engineer with deep roots in the UK and Nigerian church audio communities.

Our work is driven by a conviction that professional audio quality shouldn't be the exclusive domain of large, well-resourced venues. Smaller churches deserve the same clarity, control, and reliability.
